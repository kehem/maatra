About Thourang

Thourang, a cornerstone of MAATRA, is dedicated to preserving Manipuri heritage through visual storytelling. Our mission is to capture the essence of cultural traditions using photography, drone footage, documentaries, and exhibitions. By blending traditional narratives with modern techniques, we create compelling visual experiences that connect communities to their roots. Thourang aims to document the vibrant festivals, evolving rural landscapes, and intricate craftsmanship of Manipur, ensuring these stories resonate with future generations. Our work fosters cultural pride and awareness through carefully curated projects that honor authenticity and creativity.

Our Projects

Explore our curated projects that celebrate Manipuri heritage through visual storytelling.

Festival of Colors

This documentary captures the vibrant essence of Manipuri festivals, focusing on the intricate traditional dances and rituals that define the region's cultural identity. Through vivid cinematography, we showcase the energy of celebrations like Yaoshang and Lai Haraoba, where communities come together in colorful displays of music, dance, and devotion. The project highlights the significance of these festivals in preserving Manipuri heritage, featuring interviews with local elders and performers to provide deeper insights into their historical and cultural importance. By combining dynamic visuals with authentic narratives, Festival of Colors offers a window into the heart of Manipur’s festive spirit, fostering appreciation for its traditions. (110 words)

Village Horizons

Village Horizons is a drone-based visual project documenting the transformation of rural Manipuri villages over a decade. Using aerial footage, we capture the evolving landscapes, from traditional thatched homes to modern infrastructure, reflecting the region’s socio-economic changes. The project emphasizes the balance between preserving cultural heritage and embracing development, showcasing how villages maintain their identity amidst modernization. Interviews with residents provide personal stories of change, while stunning visuals highlight the natural beauty of Manipur’s countryside. This project aims to preserve the visual history of these communities, offering a unique perspective on their growth and resilience. (96 words)

Heritage Tales

Heritage Tales is a series of short films exploring the oral histories of Manipuri communities. Each film delves into the myths, legends, and traditions passed down through generations, capturing the voices of elders and storytellers. The project combines intimate interviews with cinematic visuals to bring these stories to life, preserving narratives that are at risk of fading. By focusing on the cultural significance of oral traditions, Heritage Tales fosters a deeper understanding of Manipur’s rich heritage, connecting younger generations with their roots through compelling storytelling. (85 words)
